ASES: Stanford's Asia launchpad

is getting ready for you

ASES is Stanford's globally focused student entrepreneurship society.

The organization was founded at Stanford University in 1998 with the goal of connecting student entrepreneurs all over the world. Today, active ASES chapters provide educational programs on entrepreneurship, design, and venture capital for students across the globe.

We challenge our members to think beyond Silicon Valley and solve global problems.
